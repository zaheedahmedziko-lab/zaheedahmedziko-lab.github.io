About

I am a Ph.D. student in Information Technology at Towson University, Maryland, USA, and a Graduate Teaching Assistant in the Department of Computer and Information Sciences. My academic and research interests include cybersecurity, AI security, IoT and cloud security, software-defined networking, secure embedded systems, and secure intelligent systems.

Previously, I completed my M.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering with a major in Cybersecurity from United International University, Bangladesh. My research background includes cloud-IoT healthcare systems, SDN security, edge intelligence, nanosatellite communication, and applied cybersecurity.
