Starting a Ph.D. journey is both challenging and exciting. At Towson University, I am continuing my academic path in Information Technology while working as a Graduate Teaching Assistant in the Department of Computer and Information Sciences.

This stage of my journey gives me the opportunity to grow as a researcher, support students through teaching-related activities, and explore deeper research questions in 5G/6G, cybersecurity, AI security, etc.

Through this blog, I plan to share selected academic updates, research reflections, teaching experiences, and personal observations from my time at Towson University.
